FANTA Rules & Guidelines

1. General Information

  • Number of Teams: 8
  • Entry Fee: $500 per team

2. Prize Structure

Prize structure now lives on the Prizes tab of the league report — buy-in pool, payout breakdown per prize, who's currently leading each one, and last season's winners are all there. The admin (via the Telegram bot) is the source of truth for any change.

3. Point System

3.1. Goals

Position Points per Goal
Forward 2
Midfield 3
Defender 4

3.2. Additional Points

  • Game Winner: 1 point * To be voted on
  • Assist: 1 point
  • Penalty Shot (scored): 2 points per player
  • Saved Penalty: 3 points
  • Tying Goal: 0.5 points

3.3. Penalties & Deductions

  • Yellow Card: -0.5 point
  • Red Card: -1 point
  • Missed Penalty: -3 points
  • Own Goal: -2 points

4. Fantasy Team Structure

  • Starting Eleven plus Bench
  • Roster Requirements:
    • 3 Goalies
    • 7 Defenders
    • 7 Midfielders
    • 5 Forwards

4.1. Fanta Score Calculation

  • Each player's Fanta score is calculated with bonuses and penalties.
  • The starting lineup's points are tallied and converted into a soccer scoreline.

4.2. Fanta Points to Score Conversion

Points Goals Scored Goals in Scoreline
65 1 1 goal
68 2 2 goals
>70 +3 points per goal after 2 Additional goals = 3 points each

4.3. Match Results & League Points

  • Win: 3 points
  • Draw: 1 point
  • Loss: 0 points

5. Winners & Tiebreakers

  • Winner: Team with the most points will host the Fanta Scudetto.
  • Tiebreakers (in order):
    • Total Points
    • Fanta Points (sum)
    • Goals For
    • Games Won
    • Goals Against

6. Additional Rules & Regulations

6.1. Roster & Lineup Regulations

  • Incomplete Roster Penalties:
    • Incomplete roster before first game of new week: -1 league point per missing player.
    • Short lineup during a game: No points for that position.
  • Lineup Omissions & Penalties:
    • 1st Game: Warning
    • 2nd/3rd Game: -1 point (current season / next season)
    • 4th Game: -2 points (current / next season)
    • 5th Game and more: -3 points (current / next season)

6.2. Player Drops & Transfer Rules

  • Max 5 drops per game week.
  • Illegal drops result in points deduction and players being re-listed.
    • All players that are deemed influenced/affected by the extra drops are void
    • Team will receive -1 league point for every extra drop
  • Dropping Traded Players:
    • Player values will stick only to the team that was last owned by.
      • if after a trade, the previous team decides to pickup the dropped player and it was last owned by the traded team. That value will stick for both teams.
        • Team A trades Buffon (100 Credits) for Dybala (100 credits) to team B. If both teams decide to drop both players, they will both have to pay those base values for each player. This eliminates collusion.
    • If a player is sold outside Serie A, no drop penalty but must drop if required.
  • Player Sold Out of Serie A:
    • Not a drop; must officially drop within 3 mercato days of warning.
  • Trade Regulations:
    • Must be announced immediately.
    • No trade Backs after finalization.
    • Trades can be protested within 24 hours.

6.3. Keeper Rules

  • One keeper from the previous season, traded or kept, at the original purchase price.
  • Goalkeepers limited to 1-year keepers.
  • Keeper duration max: 2 years.

6.4. Lineup & Transfer Penalties

  • Unmet Starting Lineup: If a team is deliberately "out of it" and not putting any effort in maintain a competitive team and they are constantly short. Penalties will be applied.
    • 2nd game in a row warning
    • 3rd game in a row
      • -1 League Point for the following year
    • 4th game or more in a row
      • -3 league points for the following year
    • Team eligibility will be reviewed for next season
